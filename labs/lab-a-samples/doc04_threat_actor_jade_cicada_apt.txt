Threat Actor Profile — APT-SG41 (Tracked: Jade Cicada)

Jade Cicada is a state-sponsored advanced persistent threat group attributed with moderate-to-high confidence to a People's Republic of China-affiliated intelligence apparatus. The group has been active since at least 2021 and focuses on long-term espionage operations targeting financial institutions, central banks, and sovereign wealth funds across Southeast Asia, with confirmed intrusion activity in Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, and Thailand. The operational objective appears to be economic intelligence collection, including pre-decisional financial policy data and cross-border capital flow information.

Initial access methods favor spear-phishing with macro-laced documents (T1566.001) and strategic web compromises targeting financial news portals frequented by the victim sector (T1189 — Drive-by Compromise). Jade Cicada maintains a custom implant family referred to internally as SILKTHREAD, a modular backdoor that communicates over HTTPS to actor-controlled infrastructure masquerading as cloud storage services. The implant supports keylogging (T1056.001), file staging (T1074.001), and tunneled SOCKS proxy capability for internal network traversal (T1090). Persistence is established via scheduled tasks and COM object hijacking (T1053.005, T1546.015).

The group demonstrates significant operational security discipline. Infrastructure is rotated frequently, with C2 domains registered through privacy-protecting registrars using cryptocurrency payments. Certificate transparency logs have been used by researchers to pivot on the group's certificate issuance patterns. Jade Cicada affiliates have exploited CVE-2025-21334 and CVE-2025-47821, affecting widely deployed network edge appliances in the ASEAN financial sector.

Recommended defensive measures include monitoring for scheduled task creation by non-administrative users, enabling enhanced logging on email gateways to capture macro-enabled document delivery, and deploying behavioral detection rules for HTTPS beaconing with regular jitter intervals, a hallmark of SILKTHREAD communications.
